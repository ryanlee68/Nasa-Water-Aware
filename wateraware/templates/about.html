ABOUT THIS PROJECT




Our Goals

Spread Awareness on information that may not be obvious to the general population.

Inform others on how they may help species in need

Warn others of possible harmful organisms and materials in the water near them






What Can You do to Help?

  1. Be mindful of what you touch(coral, dangerous animals, sensitive animals, etc) and what you put on before going into water(sun block, deodorants, soaps, etc).
  2. Keep in mind both that when entering any environment it is in a delicate balance.
  3. ABSOLUTELY NO littering; not only extremely disrespectful but also can have serious environmental consequences.
  4. When laws and regulations for environmental protection are lobbied do your part in both researching the laws affects and voting for and against them.

More on how to help can be found in the link below.

Food for Thought

  • How are ocean ecosystems and the biodiversity they support influenced by climate and environmental variability and change, and how will these changes occur over time?
  • How do carbon and other elements transition between ocean pools and pass through the Earth System, and how do biogeochemical fluxes impact the ocean and Earth's climate over time?
  • How (and why) is the diversity and geographical distribution of coastal marine habitats changing, and what are the implications for the well-being of human society?
  • How do hazards and pollutants impact the hydrography and biology of the coastal zone? How do they affect us, and can we mitigate their effects?
